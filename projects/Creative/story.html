Taken By Love

Kyren Thavorn is a powerful mafia lord by night, a respected CEO by day, and a man who has perfected the art of keeping his life neatly segmented, until a spunky, chaotic college student barges in. He meets Aiden Charoen: a confident and sassy college student, living without a care, shining brightly in a world that can't seem to keep up with him. Their first meeting is a comedy of errors, but the sparks are undeniable. Soon, their boss-and-intern relationship shifts into a thrilling slow burn, leaving Kyren with a growing desire to claim the one man who doesn't flinch in the face of his formidable presence. Kyren will burn the world for him. But when the truth of the mafia lord's life comes out, will Aiden still be standing by his side?
